About Stella

Stella Wells is a retailer of flowers and gifts in the Carmel area since 2000. With a commitment to bring the residents of Carmel and surrounding areas with superlative floral products, award winning design, and unsurpassed service.

Our unique assorment of products are mostly imported from Europe's famous flower auctions, as well as through our well established ties to the West Coast and South American growers.

You can expect outstanding delivery staff to arrive and hand deliver your gift. Every order is handled individually and guaranteed for freshness.
